Indestructo Tank

Play

Indestructo Tank offers a unique gaming experience unlike any other war game. Instead of hiding, using stealth mode, or dodging bullets, your objective is to strategically position your tank to be hit by bombs dropped by aerial vehicles, which will cause your tank to bounce. While airborne, you must skillfully maneuver your tank to collide with and destroy the enemy vehicles. All enemies in this game are airborne, adding to the challenge.

Upon launching Indestructo Tank, you will be guided through a tutorial that showcases the captivating and distinctive features of the game. Once acquainted with the gameplay mechanics, you can start driving your Indestructible tank, controlled by the left and right arrow keys, deliberately positioning it to be attacked by the aerial vehicles. It's important to note that being attacked does not result in immediate destruction; instead, it propels your tank into the air. During this airborne phase, you must navigate your tank to collide with the flying vehicles. Striking an aerial vehicle from above will trigger another bounce, allowing you to aim for multiple vehicles and achieve combo points. Each stage presents a limited amount of fuel, and flying in the air consumes fuel, so effective management is crucial for completing the stages within the given time frame.

At the end of each stage, you will be presented an experience scoreboard, reflecting the points you have earned. These experience points can be utilized to increase the frequency of enemy appearances, enabling you to engage them more frequently and stay in the game. The available enemy types for purchase include bomber, bouncer, gunner, homer, and miner. Investing in these upgrades will heighten the number of enemies encountered, enhancing your chances of defeating them, conserving fuel, and progressing to the next stage. Fuel level, experience points, and stage progress are displayed at the bottom of the screen, ensuring you are well-informed throughout your gameplay.
